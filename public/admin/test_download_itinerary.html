What the Itinerary Contains:
  • Driver Itineraries:
    • Departure time from home
    • List of addresses to pick up passengers
    • Pickup times for each passenger
    • Total travel time including overhead
  • Passenger Itineraries:
    • Name of driver who will pick them up
    • Pickup time
    • Reminder to be ready 5 minutes early
  • Summary Information:
    • Event details (name, date, time, location)
    • Total drivers and passengers
    • Important reminders for carpooling
The itinerary is generated as a text file that can be easily shared via email or messaging apps.
