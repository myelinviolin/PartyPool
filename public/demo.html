Registration Process

How the Vehicle Seats Field Works:
1 Select "Yes, I can drive" - Vehicle information section appears
2 Enter total seats in your car - This is the key field!
What We Ask

"How many total seats are in your car?"

  • Include the driver seat
  • Typical sedan = 5 seats
  • SUV = 7-8 seats
  • Minivan = 7-8 seats
How It's Used

Optimization calculates:

  • Available passenger spots = Total seats - 1
  • Groups participants efficiently
  • Minimizes total vehicles needed
  • Not all willing drivers will drive!
Example: If 10 people register and 5 are willing to drive with vehicles of 7, 5, 4, 4, and 3 seats, the system might only use the 7-seater and 5-seater (12 total capacity for 10 people), saving 3 vehicles!
